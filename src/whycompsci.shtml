Why Computer Science

As a person that is ending its third career year of Computer Science with the doubts rising about Artificial Inteligence and a shortage in internships and junior opportunities compared to past years still can be seen many reasons as to why study Computer Science.

There are a few main reasons why students choose to study Computer Science. First, technology envelopes our whole lives and many strive to contribute to that technology. Second, Computer Science is an ever-growing field with boundless opportunities still. Third, Computer Science education allows one to have a global impact via the technology that can be created.
